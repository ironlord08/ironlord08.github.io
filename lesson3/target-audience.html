Target Audience

Persona 1

Local Resident

Name: John Smith

Job Title: Salesman

Demographics:

  • 45 Years Old
  • Married
  • Father of 3
  • Loves Fishing

Goals and Tasks: Works hard at his 9 to 5 job and tries to spend as much time with his family as possible.

  • Attends children's sporting events
  • Spend time with wife
  • Go fishing

Environment: Has a hectic schedule, is competent with computers and mobile devices. Primarily browses the internet on his phone.

Quote: "I just need to know if the fish are biting."

Scenarios:

  • Needs to check the weather to see if his morning commute is affected.
  • Needs to check the weather for child's football game.
  • Needs to know if there are any events in town for the family.
  • Needs an idea for date night with his wife.
  • Needs to know if the weather is suitable for fishing.

Persona 2

Out of Town Vacationer

Name: Jane Smith

Job Title: Mother

Demographics:

  • 35 Years Old
  • Married
  • Mother of 2
  • Loves Hiking and outdoor activties

Goals and Tasks: Stay at home mom that tries to engage her kids in healthy recreational activities.

  • Attends children's sporting events
  • Spend time with husband
  • Go hiking

Environment: Stays home with young children, is competent with computers and mobile devices. Primarily browses the internet on her phone.

Quote: "I just want my kids to enjoy the great outdoors!"

Scenarios:

  • Needs to check the weather for family vacation.
  • Needs to know about activies for the family.
  • Needs to know if there are any hiking trails around.
  • Need to check the weather for clothing options.
  • Need to know about special events that could impact travel times.
