Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to inform local residents and potential visitors of the weather and ongoing activities of the area. The audience will be able to plan activities based on current events in the area as well as potential weather delays. The site will be regularly updated with real-time weather as well as a projected forecast and a monthly calender for events and activities.
