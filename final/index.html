Bienvenidos

Welcome to Scoots! Your one-stop-shop for everything scooters. We are the best in the business when it comes to getting you where you need to go on the island of Cozumel, Mexico. We are here for you to make planning your vacation a breeze. We have all the information you need to plan ahead so you can vacation in peace.

We offer a large variety of rentals to satisfy every need. Whether you want to zoom around on scooter by yourself, or take the whole family for a spin in one of our Jeeps, we have you covered. We offer Honda Metro, Dio and PCX150 scooters as well as the Honda Pioneer ATV. We also have 2 door Jeeps as well as 4 door Jeeps with air conditioning.

We are excited to help you make an unforgettable memory on one of the most beautiful islands in the world. Let us take care of you so your vacation can be stress-free and full of joy. Don't wait book today!
